About Hon. Yenge Raphael

Background, vision and commitment to Tarka Local Government Area

Background & Vision

Hon. Raphael Aondoseer Yenge, sworn in October 2024 as APC Executive Chairman of Tarka LGA, declared his candidacy on July 21, 2024, driven by a commitment to serve. With a psychology degree and 6+ years as an NGO research assistant/consultant, he's a Nigerian Psychological Association member focused on empathetic governance.

Post-tribunal clearance (April 12, 2025), his "Youth O'Clock" narrative emphasizes global best practices for planning, bridging urban-rural gaps, and cultural preservation in Benue State.

Core Commitments

  • Empowering youth and women through education and skills programs.
  • Enhancing healthcare access with facilities and donations.
  • Promoting peace, cultural events (e.g., musician celebrations Sep 16, 2025), and infrastructure.
  • Fostering worker welfare, as in recent felicitations.
