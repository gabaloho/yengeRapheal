About Hon. Yenge Raphael

Background, vision and commitment to Tarka Local Government Area

Background & Vision

Hon. Raphael Aondoseer Yenge, sworn in October 2024 as APC Executive Chairman of Tarka LGA, declared his candidacy on July 21, 2024, driven by a commitment to serve. With a psychology degree and 6+ years as an NGO research assistant/consultant, he's a Nigerian Psychological Association member focused on empathetic governance.

Post-tribunal clearance (April 12, 2025), his "Youth O'Clock" narrative emphasizes global best practices for planning, bridging urban-rural gaps, and cultural preservation in Benue State. In his first year (October 2024 - October 2025), he has delivered on 30+ landmark projects, with the historic institutionalization of BSUTH Annex being the crown achievement.

The Youth O'Clock Philosophy

A governance approach that prioritizes youth empowerment, skills development, and future-focused investments. Under this narrative, over 400 youths have been trained in various skills, 120 students received scholarships, and critical infrastructure has been modernized to global standards.

Core Commitments & Achievements

  • ✅ Empowering youth and women through education and skills programs (400+ trained)
  • ✅ Enhancing healthcare access with BSUTH Annex institutionalization
  • ✅ Promoting peace, cultural events, and infrastructure development
  • ✅ Fostering worker welfare and community engagement
  • ✅ Power restoration to communities through transformer installations
  • ✅ Rural road construction and water provision projects
  • ✅ Security improvements with reduced crime rates
  • ✅ Political inclusion through successful defections to APC
