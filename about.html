About Hon. Yenge Raphael

Background, vision and commitment to Tarka Local Government Area

Background & Vision

Elected in October 2024 as APC Chairman for Tarka LGA, Hon. Raphael Aondoseer Yenge brings a unique blend of psychology expertise and community service. With 6+ years in NGOs as a research assistant and consultant, he's a member of the Nigerian Psychological Association.

His vision: Sustainable growth through global best practices in planning, healthcare, and infrastructure for Tarka residents.

Personal Commitment

  • Empowering youth and women in local governance.
  • Bridging urban-rural divides with tech-enabled services.
  • Fostering peace and cultural heritage in Benue State.
