May 27, 2025
Children's Day Drama: A Vision of Tarka's Future

During the 2025 Children's Day celebration, students performed an inspiring drama that captured the audience's imagination. Twenty students took the stage, each representing different professions - farmers, doctors, traders, musicians, bankers, pastors, and even actors portraying societal challenges.

This powerful performance demonstrated the diverse aspirations of Tarka's youth and our commitment to nurturing every dream through the Youth O'Clock initiative.

Historic Healthcare Milestone

The first electric bike in Tarka Local Government Council has been allocated to the first Teaching Hospital in the area (BSUTH Annex), alongside the Council Ambulance. This marks a new era in emergency medical response for our communities.

Jemgbagh New Yam Festival
